Hard Chrome plating known as “Industrial Chrome” is applied to ferrous (made with iron) and nonferrous(non iron) materials to improve wear and abrasion resistance, reduce friction, prevent seizing and galling, and to restore the dimensions of undersized parts.


Hard Chrome is used in a variety of industries including: Nuclear, Aerospace, Automotive, Lawn and Garden and many more. Contact RS Enterprise to discuss your specific application.


Hard Chrome plating can be applied to all types of stainless steel alloys, most ferrous metals, and some nonferrous metals including copper and brass.
Please contact our RS Enterprise to discuss the feasibility of applying Hard Chrome .
