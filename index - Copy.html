Hard Chrome Plating

NO JOB IS TOO BIG OR TOO SMALL - WE CAN DO IT ALL

Hard Chrome plating is the process by which chromium ions dissolved in water are electrochemically transformed into chromium metal atoms and deposited uniformly across a conductive metal surface.

There are two general types of chrome plating: hard chrome plating and decorative chrome plating.

Our Hard chrome plating services are second to none, and we are proud to be the go-to hard chrome plating specialist in Pune, bhosari, chinchwad, chakan, Ahmednagar.
